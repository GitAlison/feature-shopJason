PAYMENT METHODS ARE ACCEPTED I ON THE ONLINE SHOP ?

We accept payment through Money Gram, Western Union, Wal-Mart, Cash-App, Zelle and Bitcoins. There is a 5% Discount For every Bitcoin Payment made at our online shop.

HOW LONG WILL DELIVERY TAKE ?

U.S. delivery time of 2-3 days, with Europe, Australia and Africa 3-5 days. The reasons for the delay may be due to national holidays or bad weather conditions.

Yes. An invoice is send for every purchase order made at our shop. This done to provide transparency with all our clients.
